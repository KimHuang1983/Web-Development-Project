Ōtautahi House offers rooms for 192 students in modern attractive apartments and is situated right next to our Madras Street Campus. It is close to amenities such as large supermarkets, a chemist, public transport, and is only a ten minute walk to Hagley Park. Ōtautahi House is a great place to stay whilst you are studying at Ara.


Staying at Ōtautahi House allows you to flat share with a group of students from around New Zealand and the world, make new friends and share new experiences.


You have your own room which is furnished with a king single bed, desk, chair, wardrobe, mirror and drawers.
